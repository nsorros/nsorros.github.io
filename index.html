I’ve spent over a decade making AI useful in places it had no business working — regulated industries, niche workflows, teams that’d been burned by demos that never reached production.

For the last five years I ran an independent NLP consultancy — embedded with leadership teams from seed-stage startups to FTSE-listed companies, governments, foundations and research institutions worldwide. Now I’m applying everything I learned from the outside, on the inside — as CTO and co-founder of a vertical AI startup.

My work sits at the intersection of research depth and shipping discipline. I read the papers, but I also write the migrations.
