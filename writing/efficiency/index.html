Lets write about the angle of recording the calls and use AI either

  • personalise in outreach

  • retrieve information you might have forgotten

  • review work to see if it aligns with what client asked, point out discrepancies, flag cases that re not clear so that you can raise them

  • create offers

  • draft responses based on the previous conversations and the work you have done

🍒 Here is a low hanging fruit for leveraging AI to boost your business operations

Start by recording 🎙️ and transcribing the conversations you have with your clients. Video call providers do that automatically nowadays if you enable it. Store those conversations in your cloud document storage of choice i.e. Google Drive, OneDrive, Box etc. Connect your AI tool like ChatGPT 💬 or NotionAI to your document storage. You have saved minutes if not hours from the following workflows:

📤 Personalising your communication during outreach from sales representatives

🧠 Recall relevant information from your conversations regarding your projects and current tasks

✅ Ensure you are ticking the boxes that your clients have flagged as important and also highlight areas that need more clarification

📝 Write offers to clients based on your catalog and conversations more efficiently

✉️ Draft replies informed from the work you have completed and past conversations
