🚀 Using AI to Supercharge Productivity in Human-Centered Workflows 🤖💼

At Mantis, we recently explored how AI can enhance—not replace—the thoughtful work humans do in complex processes like performance review and market analysis.

🧩 One key insight? Many teams still treat AI like a one-shot tool: throw everything into a single prompt, hoping AI magically generates the end result. But that often adds friction—errors, missed nuance, and cognitive overload from juggling too much raw output generated from the AI.

Instead, we discussed how breaking the process down into manageable, logical steps—similar to the ones humans do to progress the task—lets AI shine ✨ Let’s take market analysis as an example:

  • Extract what clients are asking from transcripts 📝

  • Find competitors that offer those services 🤺

  • Use AI to compare offerings and find strength and weaknesses 💪

  • Iterate and refine outputs in those steps by clarifying and diving deeper with AI 🤖

💫 The north star? A modular, agentic AI assistant that joins calls, scans the web, offers insights to improve market position, and becomes interactive—so teams can question, refine, and finalize the analsysis together.

This shift allows AI to become a true collaborator, not just a content generator. Over time, you get reusable prompts and workflows that mirror your team’s instincts—just faster. We’re big believers that with the right approach, AI doesn’t replace expertise—it amplifies it.
