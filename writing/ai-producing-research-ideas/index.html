💡 AI that generates novel ideas for your business

A few weeks ago, a paper was released that tested AI’s abilities to generate novel research ideas compared to experts. All ideas went into a blind review process, and AI was found to be equivalent or superior to those experts at generating research ideas 🔥

This does not mean the experts should find something else to do, and we should hand everything to AI; but it does mean that AI can be a helpful assistant in many business settings. From strategy work, all the way to implementation, businesses have an opportunity to use AI to get a competitive advantage ✨

At Mantis, we are working with many organisations, from medical and retail, to the third sector, to make the best use of generative AI.
