AI to find patients in clinical trials

based on https://www.freethink.com/health/clinical-trials-llms and https://www.medrxiv.org/content/10.1101/2024.02.08.24302376v1

https://mytomorrows.com/blog/healthcare-professionals/mytomorrows-launches-an-ai-powered-clinical-trial-search-tool/

🔍 AI is revolutionizing clinical trial matching

Finding suitable clinical trials for patients has traditionally been a complex and time-consuming process. This is partly because much of the relevant information to screen patients lives inside doctor's notes as "free text" instead of structured fields. However, recent breakthroughs in AI and Large Language Models (LLMs) are transforming how we connect patients with potentially life-saving trials 🏥

Here's why this matters:

  • ⚡️ Speed: AI can process patient records and match them against thousands of clinical trials in seconds

  • 🎯 Accuracy: LLMs can understand the medical terminology in doctor notes and make connections even in cases where the same disease or symptom is described in different ways, improving match precision

  • 📈 Efficiency: Automated screening reduces the administrative burden on healthcare providers or the cost of expensive consultants that are hired to help patients find relevant trials

Early results are promising - studies show that AI-powered systems can significantly increase the number of eligible patients identified for trials while reducing the time and resources needed for screening 📊
