We might be measuring intelligence wrong

Benchmarks reward pass@k, right at least once in k tries. But agents act once, so what matters is pass∧k, right every time.

Capability has raced ahead; reliability is climbing behind it — which is why the impressive monthly numbers don't always translate to impressive agentic performance.
