Stop using AI as a magic wand

Quick Tip: Stop Using AI as a Magic Wand

Many teams make a common mistake: dumping everything into one AI prompt and hoping for magic. This approach usually backfires, creating more work through errors and information overload.

The Better Way:

Break down your AI workflow into simple, focused steps - just like you would tackle any complex task.

Here's how to do it with market analysis:

  • First pass: Extract client requirements from call transcripts

  • Second pass: Identify relevant competitors

  • Third pass: Compare offerings and analyze strengths/weaknesses

  • Final pass: Refine insights through focused follow-up queries

The Goal:

Build towards an AI assistant that can join your meetings, research actively, and engage in real dialogue with your team to perfect the analysis together.
